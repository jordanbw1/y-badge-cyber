Cyber Lab

Welcome to the Cyber Lab! This is a simple web application that allows you to send commands to your Y-Badge. Your goal is to find and crack the password to someone's Y-Badge, and issue commands to that device.

To help you on your hacking adventure, here are the objectives you should follow:

  1. Find a "data leak" that gives you the password hash to the Y-Badge.
  2. Crack the password hash you found.
  3. Go to /control_device (or click on "Send Command" in the NavBar), fill in the IP address and password, and change the color of the Y-Badge.

Hint: A good place to start would be to navigate to "/robots.txt". Pay attention for suspicious routes, perhaps the Disallowed routes may have something of interest? ;)

Hashes Hint: Once you have the password hash, you can crack it on the website: hashes.com

Easter Eggs: If you finish the lab early, there are some easter eggs to find. Try sending GET and POST requests to a few endpoints you found in "/robots.txt". You may want to try using Postman or Curl.
